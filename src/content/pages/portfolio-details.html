Overview

Designed to lower coordination cost and improve execution visibility.

The engagement centered on a process with too many manual transfers, inconsistent status visibility, and unnecessary friction between teams. The solution introduced clearer interface logic, stronger workflow structure, and a cleaner operating rhythm.

The result was a system easier to trust, easier to manage, and easier to extend without immediately creating new process debt.
